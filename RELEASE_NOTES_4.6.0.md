# WAFD ONE 4.6.0 — Reference Master Data

Adds an idempotent migration that automatically creates a practical master-data library:

- 57 international Hajj missions.
- 35 Madinah hotels with district, address and map coordinates.
- 8 supplier categories.
- 61 food, beverage and packaging ingredients with UOM, cost and minimum stock.
- 10 warehouses: 6 operational stores and 4 cold/frozen rooms.
- 20 standardized recipes for 100 portions.
- Opening stock balances distributed by storage type.

Existing user records are preserved. Missing records are created during Migrate.
