# WAFD ONE 4.9.0

## Verified reference-data governance

- Added source, verification, and last-review fields to hotels, Hajj missions, recipes, and ingredients.
- Added central-zone classification, tourism-license, classification, and map fields for hotels.
- Added official-name, mission type, and Hajj-season fields for missions.
- Added cuisine, calories, allergens, and food-safety fields for recipes.
- Added storage-condition and shelf-life fields for ingredients.
- Added a WAFD Data Source master and registered official Ministry of Tourism, Ministry of Hajj and Umrah, and SFDA references.
- Stopped creating fabricated opening stock quantities. Inventory balances now require physical count or approved stock movement.
- Existing operational records are retained and explicitly marked by verification status rather than being presented as an exhaustive official register.
