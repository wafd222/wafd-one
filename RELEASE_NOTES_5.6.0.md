# WAFD ONE v5.6.0 — Fleet, Dispatch & Delivery Control

- Validates vehicle capacity, registration, insurance, and driver license before dispatch.
- Prevents assigning the same vehicle or driver to concurrent active trips.
- Prevents duplicate active trips for one loading record and duplicate delivery proofs.
- Enforces loading, dispatch, arrival, proof, photo, signature, quantity, and time gates.
- Synchronizes vehicle/driver availability and meal-plan delivery status.
- Adds safe complaint workflow validation and a data-repair patch for legacy statuses.
