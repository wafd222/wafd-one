# WAFD ONE v5.8.0 — Food Safety, CCP and Traceability

This release adds configurable food-safety controls, critical control point checks,
lot/expiry traceability, batch release safeguards, and a batch traceability API.
Defaults are operational starting points and must be aligned with the company's
approved HACCP plan, Saudi regulatory requirements, client contracts, and product risks.

## Main safeguards
- Configurable cooking, hot-holding, cold-holding and ambient-exposure limits.
- CCP measurements with automatic compliance calculation.
- Mandatory deviation details, corrective action and optional evidence control.
- Verified CCP records become immutable.
- Unique traceability code for every production batch.
- Food-safety release blocked until quality and CCP requirements are satisfied.
- Lot, supplier batch, production date, expiry date and receiving temperature on stock rows.
- Expired items cannot be received; invalid date and temperature combinations are rejected.
- Batch traceability endpoint consolidates materials, CCP, quality, packaging and delivery links.
